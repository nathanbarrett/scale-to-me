So just how big is the Milky Way then?

In order to get a better understanding of the vastness of the Milky Way we must first shrink down our Solar System even more. This time we will shrink it down to the size of a US Quarter.

4.2 Light Hours

At this scale it takes light 4 hours and 12 minutes to get from the center of the quarter to the outside of the quarter. The fastest man made object in space ( 14 km / second ) would take 10.18 years to get from the center of the quarter to the outside of the quarter. Refer back to this after you see the Milky Way.
